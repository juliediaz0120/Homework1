Julie Diaz: Artist Statement

In my future artistic work, I hope to expose more people to mindfulness and the idea of oneness. We all endure some type of suffering throughout our lifetime, yet we always look outwards for semblances of peace. Our different types of suffering tends to leave us feeling alone, but media can help us relate to one another and subsequently, realize our inner power to connect with each other. If more popular media portrayed more meaningful messages, such as inward peace and looking within to ease our suffering, I believe we would be a much better society.
